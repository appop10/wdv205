Who We Are

Est. 2010
Ava Anderson and Justin Smith wanted to see if they could create a unique event space in Colorado Springs

The founders saw the potential in this old, abandoned factory and wanted to share it with the rest of the city. After years of planning the reconstruction, they've created an event space that has hosted performances from Acre Husk, Chili Bars, Funnel Sixth, and many more.
