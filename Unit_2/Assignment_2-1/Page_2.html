Short Video Game 2 - Ori and the Blind Forest

Ori is a beautiful 2D platformer. I really appreciate the visuals of the different areas, and the storytelling is pretty solid. It also encourages revisiting areas once you get more abilities, which I also appreciate.

Description of the Game

The forest of Nibel is dying. After a powerful storm sets a series of devastating events in motion, an unlikely hero must journey to find his courage and confront a dark nemesis to save his home. Ori and the Blind Forest tells the tale of a young orphan destined for heroics, through a visually stunning action-platformer crafted by Moon Studios for Xbox One and PC. Featuring hand-painted artwork, meticulously animated character performance, and a fully orchestrated score, Ori and the Blind Forest explores a deeply emotional story about love and sacrifice, and the hope that exists in us all. - Description curtesy of the game's website.
